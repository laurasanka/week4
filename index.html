Chicken pasta recipe

Ingredient Amount
Pasta 500 g
Chicken fillet 300 g
Paprika 1
Whipped cream 250 ml
Onion 1
Chicken seasoning 1 sp
Oil 1 sp
Salt, pepper 1 tp
Garlic 1

Manufacture

  • Fry finely chopped peppers and onions in a large wok pan
  • If the onion is glassy, add the chicken pieces cut into small pieces
  • At the same time boil the pasta with oil and salt
  • When the chicken is cooked, season it and add the pressed garlic and pour over the cream
  • Heat until the cream approaches boiling point
  • Season the sauce if necessary
  • Drain the pasta and place it in a pan
  • Mix and serve
